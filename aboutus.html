Our History

Baked Goods by Chef Nyosi, also referred to as SHISA Bakery is a bakery established in 2014 by its founder and Head Chef, Chef Nyosi in Sterkspruit, Eastern Cape. The company started because of the love that Chef Nyosi had toward baking and his desire of serving his people with high quality and freshly baked goods. With a humble beginning, SHISA Bakery started as a small home kitchen where Chef Nyosi produced cupcakes, cakes and pastries to the friends and families as well as local customers. The quality and exclusive taste of his products spread by word of mouth fast and was the base of the gradual rise of the bakery. SHISA Bakery is an established local brand that over years established a reputation of freshness and creativity in its work and community focused service. The company remains a constituent of good taste as well as a provider of tasty baked products but also a representative of local business culture in Sterkspruit.

Mission

To deliver the people of Sterkspruit and surrounding areas with freshly baked, high-quality products that combine tradition, creativity, and passion—elevating the community while establishing a culture of excellence in baking.

Vision

To transform SHISA Bakery into one of the most outstanding bakery brands in the Eastern Cape and South Africa, synonymous with innovation, quality, and community impact.
