About SHISA Bakery

Our History

Baked Goods by Chef Nyosi, also referred to as SHISA Bakery is a bakery established in the year 2014 by its founder and Head Chef, Chef Nyosi, in Sterkspruit, Eastern Cape. The company started because of the love that Chef Nyosi had toward baking and his desire of serving his people with high quality and freshly baked goods. With a humble beginning, SHISA Bakery started as a small home kitchen where Chef Nyosi produced cupcakes, cakes and pastries for friends, families, and local customers. The quality and exclusive taste of his products spread quickly by word of mouth and became the base for the gradual rise of the bakery. SHISA Bakery is now an established local brand that has built a reputation for freshness, creativity, and community-focused service. The company remains a constituent of good taste, a provider of delicious baked products, and a proud representative of local business culture in Sterkspruit.

Mission Statement

To deliver the people of Sterkspruit and surrounding areas with products of high quality which are baked freshly, with a touch of tradition, creativity, and passion to elevate the local people and establish a culture of excellence in baking.

Vision Statement

To transform SHISA Bakery into one of the most outstanding bakery brands in both the Eastern Cape and South Africa, a brand that is synonymous with innovation, quality, and community impact, and which continues in the mission of being passionate, genuine, and rooted in hometown pride.
